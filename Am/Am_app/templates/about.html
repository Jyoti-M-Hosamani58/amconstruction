TECHNO FAB

We introduce ourselves as one of the major manufacturers of Pre Engineered Buildings (P E B) structures, Civil Construction Works & EOT Cranes & enjoying the coveted privilege of being recognized as the largest manufacturer of PEB structures in north Karnataka. We are situated at Gamangatti Industrial Area, Hubballi, Karnataka State.

Company Strength:

We come from a background rich in fabrication experience, spanning more than 25 years. We are also updated & upgraded with state-of-the-art technology. We are confident in handling all challenges related to PEB structures. (For all kinds of applications.)
