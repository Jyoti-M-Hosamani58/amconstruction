About Us

TECHNO FAB

We introduce ourselves as one of the major manufacturers of Pre Engineered Buildings (P E B) structures, Civil Construction Works & EOT Cranes & enjoying the coveted privilege of being recognized as the largest manufacturer of PEB structures in the north Karnataka. We are situated at Gamangatti Industrial Area, Hubballi, Karnataka State.

Company Strength:

We are coming from a back ground of a rich experience of fabrication, existing since more than 25 years in this field. We are also updated & upgraded with state of the art technology. We are confident of handling all the challenges of PEB structures. (For all kind of Applications)

We design, manufacture & carry out erection at site for simple to highly sophisticated projects in any part of India. We also undertake “TURNKEY” projects including civil works. In a nutshell, we provide to customers “END TO END SOLUTIONS”.

We appreciate our valued customers for all the support given till date because of which we are existing strongly with the great pride today.
